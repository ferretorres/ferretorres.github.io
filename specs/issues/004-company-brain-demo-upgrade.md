# Upgrade: Company Brain guided demo visual

## Problem

The Company Brain concept is central to the positioning, but the demo page needs to show the concept as an operational system.

## User story

As a CEO, CTO, or COO, I want to see how company data becomes an AI-ready operating layer, so I can understand what the Company Brain would do in my organization.

## Scope

- Upgrade `demo-company-brain.html`.
- Add a synthetic Company Brain screen showing sources, retrieval, memory, permissions, dashboards, and assistant output.
- Show input sources such as docs, tickets, meetings, dashboards, and policies.
- Show output types such as answers with sources, decision memory, workflow recommendations, and executive visibility.
- Add clear "synthetic guided preview" labeling.

## Acceptance criteria

- Above-the-fold section includes a strong visual preview.
- The page explains inputs, AI layer, human controls, and outputs.
- CTA invites visitors to request a guided demo meeting.
- No client-specific data or fabricated client claims are used.
- Mobile view remains clean.

- Confirm CTA works.
