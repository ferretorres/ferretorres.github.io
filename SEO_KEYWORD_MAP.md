## Homepage keyword intent

The homepage should not try to rank for every long-tail query. It should reinforce the two strongest commercial terms:

- AI consultancy
- AI implementation services
- AI systems for companies
- RAG, agents, Company Brain, finance intelligence
- Netherlands and Europe

## Wording rules

- Use "AI implementation services" and "AI systems implementation" when the buyer wants a builder.
- Use "AI consultancy" and "AI consulting" together where natural, because searchers use both.
- Use "implementation partner" for enterprise and CTO pages.
- Keep "Company Brain" as the differentiating concept, but anchor it to concrete terms: private company knowledge, RAG, agents, dashboards, workflow automation.
- Avoid keyword stuffing in the hero. Put exact terms in title, description, H1/H2, internal links, FAQ, and schema.
