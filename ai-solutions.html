Demo roadmap

Interactive demos will be added when they are useful, not as decorative prototypes.

  • Each demo should represent a real business workflow and a possible implementation path.
  • Demo data will be synthetic or anonymized; no client data is needed for public previews.
  • The goal is to help leadership and technical teams understand scope before a build starts.
