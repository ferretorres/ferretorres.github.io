Engagement model

Prototype first, then scale the integration layer.

Engagements can start with a narrow MVP or proof-of-concept around one workflow where AI changes the economics. If the system proves useful with real users, data, and constraints, the team expands it into production infrastructure.

AI opportunity assessment AI integration roadmap AI consulting process AI MVP and PoC development
  1. Select

    Choose one high-value workflow with clear data, owners, risks, and decision loops.

  2. Prototype

    Ship a working MVP or PoC with RAG, dashboards, assistants, and governance where needed.

  3. Validate

    Measure usefulness, speed, accuracy, adoption, and workflow fit before committing to the full build.

  4. Scale

    Turn the working system into shared infrastructure, reusable assistants, or internal SaaS.

AI consulting questions

Questions companies ask before starting AI-first development.

How should a company start with AI consulting?

Start with one high-value workflow where the users, data, bottleneck, and business result are clear enough to validate quickly.

Can the project begin as an MVP or PoC?

Yes. A focused MVP or PoC is often the right way to test usefulness, accuracy, workflow fit, and adoption before scaling the system.

What can Ferre Torres B.V. build?

Company Brain platforms, RAG assistants, agentic workflows, finance dashboards, project intelligence, reporting automation, and AI-native tools.

Does AI-first development replace existing tools?

Not by default. The usual goal is to connect current systems and add an AI-ready operating layer around data, workflows, and decisions.
