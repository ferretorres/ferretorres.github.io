Engagement routes

Start at the level of clarity the company already has.

  • AI opportunity assessment: when many AI ideas exist but the first build is unclear.
  • AI integration roadmap: when workflow priorities exist but architecture and sequencing need structure.
  • AI MVP or PoC: when one workflow, owner, users, and data path are ready to validate.
  • AI architecture review: when an AI prototype or plan needs a production path.
  • Guided demo: when stakeholders need to align around what the system could look like.
  • Enterprise AI RFP checklist: when procurement or leadership needs clearer partner evaluation criteria.
